Android community is best known for aftermarket software development. Most of the OEMs fail to provide proper support from the software side after the release of their product. Here arises the need for Custom ROMS and Custom Kernels. I try to provide aftermarket support for Asus Zenfone Max Pro M1 (Codenamed as X00TD/X00T), Xiaomi Redmi Note 4 (Codenamed as mido) and Lenovo K6 series (Codenamed as karate for K6 and K6 Power, karatep as K6 Note).

Products

Custom ROMS and kernels made by me mainly concentrates on performance without compromising battery backup. Balanced profile is used across my works. Since all the works are opensource, anyone can check and evaluate the progress through my github. Roms and kernels will be always upto date as long as google and caf releases their sources. Faster security updates is one of the other features. Enjoy the projects.
